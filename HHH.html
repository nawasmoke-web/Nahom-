About HOH

Hanna Orphans Home (HOH) is a charity organization based in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. Founded in 1994, HOH provides shelter, food, education, healthcare, and emotional support to vulnerable children and orphans.

Our Programs

Education

School support, tutoring classes, and library access.

Healthcare

Medical support and counseling for children.

Community Support

Helping widows, elderly people, and vulnerable families.
